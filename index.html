ABOUT ME

Web Developer | Fashion Designer | Photographer | Traveler

I am a full stack web developer with a passion for visual arts and design. My background is in Merchandising and Fashion Design, which satisfied my desire for a creative, design-oriented work, however I also desired a career that was equally analytical. My love for programming was discovered after stumbling upon an online coding course and after writing just a few lines of code, I was hooked! Although my love for programming seems very distant from my fashion background, it satiates my need for both creative and analytical work. I was very lucky to have had the opportunity to attend Flatiron School where I was able to dive deeper into this field with a community of people that truly inspires me. My fashion background coupled with my experience in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React allows me to use my design skills with web development to create beautiful and user-friendly web apps.

In addition to my fashion and tech work, I am a musician. I have been playing violin and piano for many years. I'm a lover of all things outdoors: hiking, camping, rock climbing, kayaking, white water rafting, sky diving, swimming...(I pretty much love it all). And I'm an avid traveler, and a novice travel/nature photographer. Feel free to check out my work on Instagram: @abbywan.kenobi

UnTitled
UnTitled is an app to assist writers with writer's block by providing writing prompts, searchable by popularity or genre. Users can submit their own prompts, or write stories from other user's prompts, as well as like and comment stories and prompts.

Demo | GitHub


Algorhythm

This app creates tailor made playlists using the Spotify API. Users can input song titles, artist names, or genres, which will then generate a random playlist based off of those selections, which can then be uploaded and saved to a Spotify account. Users can then view other user's playlists and comment on them.
