ABOUT ME

Web Developer | Fashion Designer | Photographer | Traveler

I am a full stack web developer with a passion for visual arts and design. My background is in Merchandising and Fashion Design, which satisfied my desire for a creative, design-oriented work, however I also desired a career that was equally analytical. My love for programming was discovered after stumbling upon an online coding course and after writing just a few lines of code, I was hooked! Although my love for programming seems very distant from my fashion background, it satiates my need for both creative and analytical work. I was very lucky to have had the opportunity to attend Flatiron School where I was able to dive deeper into this field with a community of people that truly inspires me. My fashion background coupled with my experience in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React allows me to use my design skills with web development to create beautiful and user-friendly web apps.
